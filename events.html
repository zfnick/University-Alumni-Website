MY GROUPMATE IS NOT DOING ANYTHING

Have you ever experienced working in a group where your group mates are not contributing? And then you end up having to do the whole group assignment by yourself?

Well, if that is so, come attend this webinar by the Bachelor of Psychology, Year 3 students and we will tell you the psychology behind group work and achieve success !!️

We have invited, Dr. Junaidah Yusof, senior lecturer at University Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), who is an expert in organisational behaviour. In her expertise, this will help students to learn more in depth on the topic of this webinar.

Date: 16th February 2022, Wednesday
Time: 4.00 pm to 5.00 pm
Platform: Zoom
1 CCA hour will be provided

Any inquiries may contact, Nina (01139523242)

HOBBY TALK

Hobby Talk is a 1-hour online platform that encourages students to share their talents as well as hobbies. Having hobbies allows a person to destress themselves as well as giving the opportunity to be more productive in promoting a better lifestyle.
