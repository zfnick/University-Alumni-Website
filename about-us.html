Welcome to Quest International University!

Hi there! We are a group which consists of 3 members, Evan, Nicholas, and Qian Ni. This is our group assignment and we are determined to create an alumni website for Quest International University.

The main objective of this website is to connect all the Quest community, including the alumni, students and lecturers. We allow everyone to share their thoughts, promote their events, and also provide career opportunity to Quest community.

We hope that this website will be launched successfully, so that our alumni will have a platform to stay connected with each other!
